# AI Consulting Site Art-Direction Decision

Date: 2026-07-18

The concept pass used the buyer contract, real portfolio evidence, the GPT-5.6 Sol research rubric, and the existing site constraints. Concepts were compared before the production direction was implemented.

## Direction A — Operational Ledger

An evidence-first consulting site built from dark operating sections, numbered decisions, rules, and status-led proof. Strong on control and technical credibility, but at risk of feeling like internal software rather than a personal service.

Score: 78/100.

Rejected as the complete direction. Retained selectively for the dark buyer-situation and safe-method sections where sequence and control matter.

## Direction B — Founder Editorial

A highly personal, publication-like site led by portrait, long-form point of view, large serif typography, and narrative case studies. Strong on warmth and accountability, but slower at explaining the engagement path and harder to scan for an operations buyer.

Score: 82/100.

Rejected as the complete direction. Retained selectively through serif hierarchy, natural portraiture, generous whitespace, and direct first-person copy.

## Direction C — Founder-Led Operations Studio

A quiet professional-services system combining immediate proof, recognizable workflow situations, a three-step engagement ladder, concise case evidence, production safeguards, and direct founder accountability. Warm off-white and ink carry the editorial layer; restrained blue marks action; dark operating sections create useful contrast without becoming a dashboard.

Score: 92/100.

Selected because it best answers the buyer’s ten-second decision while preserving the strongest traits of the existing proof dossier.

## Rejection list

- Decorative gradients, glow, glass, floating illustrations, and abstract AI motifs.
- Generic equal-weight feature cards.
- Tool-stack-first copy.
- Anonymous agency “we” language.
- Invented metrics or testimonials.
- A free-form contact path competing with the workflow-audit journey.
- Desktop layouts compressed into mobile without a one-decision-at-a-time hierarchy.

## Approved reference contract

The implemented homepage is the reference for 390×844, 768×1024, and 1440×1000. Required states include mobile navigation open/closed, English/French content, reduced motion, contact idle/error/success, audit idle/error/success, representative proof, and long localized copy.
