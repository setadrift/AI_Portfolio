# First 10 Trade Lead Cold Email Drafts - 2026-06-22

## Research Notes

Best-practice pattern used for these drafts:

- Keep the first email short, plain text, and specific.
- Reference one real detail from the business, but do not overdo flattery.
- Lead with an operational problem, not "AI consulting."
- Offer a concrete first project: intake, quote follow-up, dispatch, maintenance reminders, job-status tracking, or bid/spec review.
- Use one low-friction call to action.
- For Canada, include a clear unsubscribe line.
