Review the revised Willow Grey data-entry intake build spec.

Do not propose a broad platform rebuild. The goal is to pressure-test whether
this is the safest and most credible follow-up prototype direction.

  should make the independent-contractor approach feel focused and practical.

Decision question:

Is this data-entry intake spec the safest, most credible prototype direction for
Willow Grey, and what should be changed before implementation?

Required output, in this order:

Verdict:

- Give 2-3 sentences explaining the verdict.

- Exactly 4 bullets.
- Tie each bullet to Lucy / Willow Grey leadership reading the prototype.

Highest-value revisions:

- Each bullet must be specific enough to edit the spec or page from it.
- Focus on reducing assumptions, improving clarity, and making the demo easier
  to understand.

Scope guardrails:
- List what the prototype must not imply.
- Include database/source-of-truth, live integrations, and automatic writes.

Recommended demo shape:

- State the extracted fields to show.

Copy improvements:
- Suggest a better headline if useful.
- Suggest a better supporting paragraph if useful.
- Suggest the best follow-up email framing line.

Implementation readiness:
- Say whether the spec is ready to hand to Codex for implementation.
- Name any missing pieces that would block implementation.

Missing evidence:

Budget:
- Target 900-1,200 words.
- Be candid and practical.
- Do not end mid-sentence.
